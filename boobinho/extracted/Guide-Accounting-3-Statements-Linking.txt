+ Cash & Cash -Equivalents  
+ Accounts Receivable  
+ Inventory  
+ Prepaid Expenses  
 
Long -Term Assets:  
+ Financial Investments  
+ Goodwill  
+ Net PP&E  
+ Other Intangible Assets  
+ Net Deferred Tax Asset  
+ Operating Lease Assets  
= Total Assets  
 
Current Liabilities:  
+ Accounts Payable  
+ Accrued Expenses  
+ Deferred Revenue  
 
Long -Term Liabilities:

+ Preferred Stock

 Cash Flow Statement  
Cash Flow from Operations:  
+ Net Income  to Common  
+ Depreciation  
+ Amortization  
+ Stock -Based Comp.  
+ PP&E  Write -Down  
+ Goodwill Impairment  
+ Losses / ( –) Gains  
(+ / –) Deferred Income Taxes  
(+ / –) Change in Operating Lease Assets  
(+ / –) Change in Operating Lease Liabilities  
Changes in Working Capital : 
(+ / –) Change in Accounts Receivable  
(+ / –) Change in Invent ory 
(+ / –) Change in Prepaid Expenses  
(+ / –) Change in Accounts Payable  
(+ / –) Change in Accrued Expenses  
(+ / –) Change in Deferred Revenue  
= Cash Flow from Operations  (CFO)  
 
Cash Flow from Investing:  
– Capital Expenditures  
– Buy / (Sell) Financial Investments  
+ PP&E Sale Proceeds  
= Cash Flow from Investing  (CFI)  
 
Cash Flow from Financing:  
+ Issue / (Repay) Debt  
+ Issue / (Repay) Finance Leases  
+ Issue / (Repay) Preferred Stock  
+ Issue / (Repurchase) Common Shares  
– Issue Common Dividends  
= Cash Flow from Financing  (CFF)  
Net Change in Cash = CFO + CFI + CFF  
Ending Cash = Beg. Cash + Net Change

 Accounting Rules  
 
Cash Flow (CF) Rules  
Asset UP → Cash Flow  DOWN  
Asset DOWN  → Cash Flow  UP 
 
Liability UP → Cash Flow  UP 
Liability DOWN  → Cash Flow  DOWN  
 
Cash Flow from Operations includes 
Net Income , Non -Cash Adjustments , 
and changes to Current Assets 
Excluding Cash and Investments  and 
changes to Current Liabilities 
Excluding Debt . 
 
Cash Flow from Investing includes 
changes to  many  Long -Term Assets  
and also non-operational Current 
Assets , such as short -term 
investments.  
 
Cash Flow from Financing includes 
changes to many Long -Term 
Liabilities  and Equity  items, and 
also non-operational Current 
Liabilities , such as short -term d ebt. 
 
Record Revenue  and Expenses  only 
in the period when the associated 
product or service is delivered . If 
there’s no delivery , use Balance 
Sheet items like Deferred Revenue 
and Prepaid Expenses to track 
these items.
